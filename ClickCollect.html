Click & Collect

How Does Click & Collect work?

Click & Collect is the perfect last minute shopping option, allowing you the convenience of shopping online without the shipping costs, and you can collect in store within 2 trading hours. And If you can’t come in straight away, your Click & Collect order will be held for up to 14 days.

Here’s how simple it is to shop via Click & Collect:

  • Visit our Click & Collect Site
  • Select your local Priceline store to begin shopping
  • Shop by brand or category, and simply add products to your cart
  • Select Click & Collect as your delivery method
  • Relax and wait to be notified when your order is ready for collection!

The delivery costs are flat rate regardless of volume, weight or destination. If the total cost of your order is over $50, delivery is free (Regular delivery only).
