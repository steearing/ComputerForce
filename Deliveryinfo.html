Delivery information

What are the delivery fees and options for online purchases?

  • Our delivery costs are:
  • Free standard shipping for orders over $50
  • Standard shipping is $9.95 for orders under $50
  • Express delivery as an option is temporarily unavailable due to the online order delays. We apologise for any inconvenience caused.

The delivery costs are flat rate regardless of volume, weight or destination. If the total cost of your order is over $50, delivery is free (Regular delivery only).
